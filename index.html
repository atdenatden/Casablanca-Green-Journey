CasablancaGreen Journey
The programme

Small choices, shared impact.

Green Journey is the sustainability rewards programme by Casablanca, used by independent hotels across the DACH region. It rewards the green choices you already make on holiday — skipping a cleaning, reusing towels, arriving by train. No forms, no effort. Your choices are tracked automatically and turned into Green Credits.

🍃
Earn effortlessly

Every green choice during your stay quietly adds credits — tracked through your room key.

🔄
Credits travel with you

Your balance works across 100+ independent Green Journey hotels & partners in Germany, Austria & Switzerland — earn them at one, spend them at another.

🎁
Spend on what you love

Redeem at the spa, bar, local partners — or plant a tree and watch your impact grow.

CasablancaGreen Cup
The competition

Casablanca Green Cup.

Every year, the independent hotels powered by Casablanca compete on the total Green Credits their guests earn. The greener the guests, the higher the hotel climbs. Scores are adjusted for capacity, so small properties compete fairly with the big ones.
